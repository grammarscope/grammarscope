Overview
  • GrammarScope uses SyntaxNet as its parse engine.
  • GrammarScope is not meant to parse texts beyond sentence boundaries.
  • Sentence boundaries are not detected. Use newline to manually force a new sentence, or use GrammarScope's OpenNLP sentence boundary detector, for English and French only, which will do just that.
  • Semantic features are meant to simplify dependency structures and retain only 5 types of relations.
    Predicate Predicates or relations
    Subject Subjects in a relation
    Object Objects of a relation
    Term-modifying
    Predicate    		 Sub-predicates that modify a term
    Predicate-modifying
    Predicate    		 Sub-predicates that modify a predicate
